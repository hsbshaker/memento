# Memento Landing Page – Redlines

## Container
- Max width: ~1200–1280px
- Centered layout

---

## Hero Section

### Padding
- Top: 120–160px
- Bottom: 100–140px

---

### Headline
- Size: ~88–104px
- Line height: tight
- Max width: ~600px

---

### Subtext
- Size: ~18–22px
- Max width: ~480px
- Spacing below headline: ~24px

---

### CTA Button
- Height: 60–72px
- Padding: large horizontal
- Rounded (pill shape)
- Prominent blue fill
- Subtle glow

---

### Card Pills
- Height: ~32–36px
- Rounded
- Low contrast background
- Slight hover elevation

---

### Product Visual Panel
- Large rounded container (~20–28px radius)
- Very subtle border or none
- Soft shadow/glow behind
- Slightly elevated from background

---

### Floating Callouts
- Small rounded cards
- Soft shadow
- Positioned partially outside main panel

---

## How It Works Section

### Spacing
- Top margin: ~120px
- Between header and steps: ~60px

---

### Step Cards
- Equal width columns
- Large numbers (01, 02, 03)
- Light dividers or subtle separation
- Generous internal padding (~40px+)

---

## Final CTA Section

### Container
- Centered block
- Rounded edges
- Subtle border or glow

---

### Headline
- Large (~56–72px)
- Center aligned

---

### CTA Button
- Same style as hero CTA
- Centered

## Visual Style

### Background
- Dark base
- Subtle gradient (blue → yellow hints)

### Shadows
- Soft, diffused
- Never harsh

### Borders
- Extremely subtle or none

---

## Key Principle

Everything should feel:
- light
- spaced out
- premium
- intentional

Nothing should feel cramped or template-like.
